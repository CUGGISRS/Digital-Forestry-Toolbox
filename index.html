Digital-forestry-toolbox

A collection of digital forestry tools for Matlab

About

The Digital Forestry Toolbox is collection of tools and tutorials for Matlab designed to help process data (e.g. remote sensing and field surveys) related to forests.

Setup

  1. Download and decompress the Zip or Tar archive
  2. Open any of the functions in Matlab and read the examples in the header
